Some thoughts on why the five-domain multisite collapsed into a single
andresanz.com — kept private for now because it's mostly half-baked.

The short version: maintaining five Node services, five Apache vhosts, and five
sets of branding for one person's writing turned into more overhead than the
distinct identities were worth. One site, paths instead of domains, much simpler.
