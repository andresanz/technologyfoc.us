This is the first post on the new andresanz.com — a single Node app, no Apache,
running on Hono and serving everything that used to live across five domains.

The whole stack is one process: TLS terminates in Node, content is markdown on
disk, images live on S3 with a Sharp resize layer, and the editor in the admin
panel runs CodeMirror with vim mode.

## What's here

- Posts (this is one)
- Pages (try `/about`)
- A private area at `/private`

Everything under `/private/*` requires a password. Everything else is public.
