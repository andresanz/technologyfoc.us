If you can read this, the auth gate worked.

Private posts live in `content/private/posts/` and are served at `/private/posts/`.
They never appear in public listings, RSS, or sitemaps. The `noindex` header is
set by the auth middleware itself so it carries through to all responses under
`/private`.
